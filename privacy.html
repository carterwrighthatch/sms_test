Privacy Policy

Hatch Stamping Company LLC is committed to protecting your privacy. When you submit your name and phone number via our SMS consent form, we use that information solely to send you important updates related to machine status, robot faults, and maintenance events.

We do not sell, share, or disclose your personal information to third parties. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any SMS message.

Your data is stored securely and only for as long as necessary to provide the messaging services you've opted into.

If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy, please contact us directly.
